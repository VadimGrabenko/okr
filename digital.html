Digital Library

A digital library (also called an online library) is an online database...

Objects in a digital library can consist of:

  • Digitized content, such as printed books, photographs, or historical manuscripts.
  • Originally produced digital content, including word processor files or social media posts.

In addition to storing content, digital libraries provide tools for organizing, searching, and retrieving materials within the collection. Their size and scope can vary greatly, ranging from small personal archives to extensive databases maintained by organizations.

The digital content may be stored locally or accessed remotely via computer networks. These information retrieval systems ensure interoperability and sustainability, allowing them to exchange information with one another.
